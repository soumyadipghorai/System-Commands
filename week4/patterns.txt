Aadhar card number contains 12 digits and can look like 123456781234 for example. Pincodes of cities in India contain 6 digits and that of IITM is 600036.

Phone numbers without the country code or o prefix for std code are 10 digits. An example for my office landline is 4422574770 and prefix with o to dial me in my office Roll numbers in IIT for regular students are of the pattern MM22B001 where the first two letters correspond to the Department code, 2 digits for the year of joining, then the program code character and then a 3 digit number for ther roll number within the class.
